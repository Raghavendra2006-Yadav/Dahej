About This Project

This project is a satirical take on the concept of dowry in Indian society. By "calculating" dahej based on arbitrary traits, we highlight the absurdity of assigning monetary value to humans.

We hope this tool makes you think, laugh, and reflect. Let's work together to end dowry culture.
